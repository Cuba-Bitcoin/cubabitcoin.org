What is Kmbalache?

Kmbalache is a Mostro instance operated by the Cuba Bitcoin community.

Mostro is a P2P exchange protocol that runs over Nostr, designed to enable buying and selling Bitcoin in a decentralized way, with no custody, no intermediaries and no KYC, preserving user privacy.

With Kmbalache you can trade sats for local currency directly with other people, using Lightning Network for fast, efficient and low-cost transactions.

Just like Bitcoin, Kmbalache has no borders: anyone, from anywhere in the world, can buy or sell Bitcoin using their local currency. We invite you to use our Mostro instance and become part of a truly free market.

Step-by-step guide

Set up Kmbalache as your Mostro instance in just 8 steps:

1

Open Mostro and go to the side menu

2

Select "Settings"

3

Find the "Mostro" section at the bottom

4

Tap "Select Mostro node"

5

Find "Kmbalache" in the list

6

Confirm the Kmbalache selection

7

Verify that Kmbalache is active

8

Done! Start trading on Kmbalache
